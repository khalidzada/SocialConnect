1. Introduction
Purpose:

This document outlines the schedule, key milestones, resource allocation, and risk management strategies for SocialConnect+.

Objectives:
  • Define timelines, milestones, and responsibilities.
  • Identify risks and mitigation plans.
  • Map dependencies for smooth project execution.
2. Project Schedule & Timeline
Overview:

The project is structured into phases, each with key deliverables. Below is the high-level schedule:

Phase 1: Planning & Requirements
  • Finalize SRS and Design Documents.
  • Conduct Stakeholder meetings and approvals.
  • Risk assessment and resource planning.
Phase 2: Design & Prototyping
  • Create detailed design and prototypes.
  • Establish system architecture and integration points.
3. Milestones & Critical Checkpoints
Key Milestones:
  • M1: Requirements Finalization (End of Week 4)
  • M2: Design Approval (End of Week 8)
  • M3: Core Module Completion (Week 12)
  • M4: E-Commerce Module Completion (Week 16)
  • M5: Full System Integration & Testing (Week 24)
  • M6: Go-Live & Post-Launch Review (Week 26)
Critical Checkpoints:
  • Weekly sprint reviews and bi-weekly stakeholder meetings.
  • Monthly risk review sessions to mitigate issues early.
4. Resource Allocation
Human Resources:
  • Project Manager: Oversees project and communications.
  • Technical Lead: Guides system architecture and integrations.
  • Development Team: Works on core functionalities.
  • QA/Test Engineers: Ensure quality through testing.
  • UI/UX Designers: Focus on user experience and interface.
Technical & Financial Resources:
  • Software Tools: IDEs, project management tools, version control.
  • Hardware: On-premises servers, cloud hosting.
  • Budget: Allocation for personnel, licenses, and hardware.
5. Risk Management Strategies
Identified Risks:
  • Schedule delays due to requirements or sprint slippage.
  • Resource constraints and budget issues.
  • Integration challenges with third-party services.
Mitigation Strategies:
  • Buffer periods for unexpected delays.
  • Regular reviews to address issues promptly.
  • Detailed risk log for tracking and mitigating issues.
6. Dependencies
Internal Dependencies:
  • Completion of SRS and design documents before development.
  • Resource availability for each sprint.
External Dependencies:
  • Payment gateway integrations with Stripe, PayPal.
  • Third-party OAuth services for user authentication.
7. Conclusion

This roadmap provides a comprehensive plan to guide the development and successful deployment of SocialConnect+. By addressing risks and mapping dependencies early, the project ensures smooth execution from start to finish.
