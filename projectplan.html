Project Plan / Roadmap for SocialConnect+

1. Introduction
Purpose

This Project Plan / Roadmap defines the project’s schedule, key milestones, resource allocation, risk management strategies, and dependencies. It ensures all stakeholders have a clear understanding of the timeline and critical checkpoints before development of SocialConnect+ begins.

Objectives
  • Establish clear timelines and deadlines for project phases.
  • Define critical milestones and checkpoints.
  • Allocate resources and assign responsibilities.
  • Identify potential risks and outline mitigation strategies.
  • Map dependencies between internal tasks and external integrations.
2. Project Schedule & Timeline
Overview

The project is divided into multiple phases with defined deliverables and deadlines. Below is a high-level view of the project schedule:

  • Phase 1: Planning & Requirements (Weeks 1-4): Finalize SRS and Design Documents, stakeholder meetings, and risk assessment.
  • Phase 2: Design & Prototyping (Weeks 5-8): Develop Detailed Design Documents, wireframes, prototypes, and establish preliminary system architecture.
  • Phase 3: Development (Weeks 9-20):
    • Sprint 1 (Weeks 9-12): Core functionalities – Authentication and Basic Social Features.
    • Sprint 2 (Weeks 13-16): E-Commerce Module, Virtual Shops, and Payment Integration.
    • Sprint 3 (Weeks 17-20): Discovery, Recommendations, and Advanced Social Features.
  • Phase 4: Testing & Quality Assurance (Weeks 21-24): Unit, integration, system testing, performance, security, and user acceptance testing.
  • Phase 5: Deployment & Go-Live (Weeks 25-26): Final deployment, post-launch monitoring, support, and documentation handover.

Note: Timeline subject to adjustments based on feedback and emerging project needs.

3. Milestones & Critical Checkpoints
Key Milestones
  • M1: Requirements Finalization (End of Week 4): SRS and initial design documents approved.
  • M2: Design & Prototype Approval (End of Week 8): Prototypes validated and stakeholder sign-off secured.
  • M3: Completion of Core Module Development (End of Sprint 1, Week 12): Core authentication and social features functional.
  • M4: E-Commerce Module Completion (End of Sprint 2, Week 16): Virtual shop setup and payment integrations complete.
  • M5: Full System Integration & Testing (End of Week 24): All modules integrated; testing successfully completed.
  • M6: Go-Live & Post-Launch Review (Week 26): System deployed, operational, and initial support phase concluded.
Critical Checkpoints
  • Weekly sprint reviews to assess progress.
  • Bi-weekly stakeholder meetings for feedback and course corrections.
  • Monthly risk review sessions.
4. Resource Allocation
Human Resources
  • Project Manager: Oversees timelines, resource allocation, and stakeholder communication.
  • Technical Lead/Architect: Guides system architecture and integration.
  • Development Team: Frontend and backend developers with expertise in .NET Core, C#, and Bootstrap.
  • QA/Test Engineers: Perform unit, integration, and performance testing.
  • UI/UX Designers: Design and iterate on user interface layouts and prototypes.
  • DevOps Engineer: Manages CI/CD pipelines and deployment processes.
  • Support/Documentation Team: Provides ongoing support and documentation.
Technical & Financial Resources
  • Software Tools: IDEs (Visual Studio), project management tools (JIRA, Trello), version control (Git).
  • Hardware: On-premises servers for initial hosting, future migration to cloud environments (Azure/AWS).
  • Budget Allocation: Personnel, software licenses, hardware procurement, and contingency funds.
5. Risk Management Strategies
Identified Risks
  • Schedule Delays: Potential delays in requirements or development sprints.
  • Resource Constraints: Limited availability of key personnel or budget overruns.
  • Integration Issues: Challenges integrating external APIs (payment gateways, ML.NET).
  • Technical Challenges: Unforeseen technical issues or compatibility problems.
  • Compliance & Security Risks: Risks related to GDPR, PCI DSS, and data encryption.
Mitigation Strategies
  • Contingency Planning: Buffer periods within phases to address unexpected delays.
  • Regular Reviews: Weekly and bi-weekly progress meetings to address issues early.
  • Risk Log: Maintain a detailed log of risks with mitigation steps.
  • Resource Flexibility: Cross-train team members and secure backup resources.
  • Vendor/Partner Communication: Regular check-ins with external service providers.
6. Dependencies
Internal Dependencies
  • Completion of SRS and Design Documents before starting development.
  • Sequential progression from design to development and testing.
  • Availability of development and QA teams during sprint cycles.
External Dependencies
  • Payment Gateways: Timely integration with Stripe and PayPal APIs.
  • ML.NET Libraries: Dependence on ML.NET for recommendation algorithms.
  • Third-Party Services: External OAuth providers (Google, Facebook) for authentication.
  • Cloud Migration: Future migration dependent on external hosting support (Azure/AWS).
7. Conclusion
Summary

This Project Plan / Roadmap provides a clear timeline, establishes key milestones, and outlines resource allocation and risk management strategies for SocialConnect+. By defining internal and external dependencies, it lays a strong foundation for the successful development and deployment of the platform.
